What are Solar Panels?

The solar energy obtained through photovoltaic solar panels originates by transforming the energy from the sun, photons, into electricity. Photovoltaic solar energy, being a modern and renewable energy source, has an impact on: The provision of electrical energy, on the economy of the Countries and contributes to caring for the environment by reducing the generation of CO2. The varied offer of solar panels that exists in the market allows photovoltaic solar kits to be within the reach of all people, whether for use in homes, residences or homes that are committed to clean energy and want to save on the electricity bill or either companies or people who do not have the possibility of an electricity distribution company supplying them with electricity in their community, sector or environment. The production of electricity through photovoltaic solar panels is possible thanks to the materials with which they were manufactured. Solar cell technology has improved over time and today the market offers products with a longer lifespan and excellent reliability. Currently, solar panels are on average 40% of the cost of a complete on-grid photovoltaic installation and are directly responsible for 100% of the energy production of each photovoltaic solar installation.

The Pros and Cons of Solar Energy for use at home or business

There are several pros and cons to consider when choosing a solar panel installation. The first thing to think about is whether you are considering installing a residential system or more of a commercial type. Residential systems tend to be smaller than commercial systems and generally produce less power. Commercial systems are often larger and more powerful, and can provide enough electricity to power multiple homes or an industry, or even sell power to a community or utility company. Installing solar panels on your home or business is a great way to reduce your energy costs and cleanly generate electricity for your family or community. Solar PV systems come in many different types, so you'll need to choose the one that's right for your power needs. Here it is best to seek advice from an expert in renewable energy like us to get the most out of your investment.

Component Life

The components of solar installations are characterized for the most part by their long useful life, solar panels reach a useful life of more than 30 years and with 25-year manufacturer guarantees.
